About TasteConnect

Our Story

TasteConnect was born from a simple observation: students love discovering new food and beverage products, and start-ups desperately need real, honest, and affordable consumer feedback. We wanted to create a space where these two worlds could meet in a dynamic, social, and playful way.

What Problem We Solve

Many early-stage F&B brands struggle to reach the right audience, collect reliable feedback, and build visibility without expensive marketing. TasteConnect solves this by offering start-ups a direct testing platform and students an affordable, interactive tasting experience.
